Power

  • Bueno de Mesquita & Smith 2022: The Dictator's Handbook
    • Nominal selectorate: those who have the right to have a say
    • Real selectorate: those who actually cast a vote
    • Winning coalition: those whose votes produce victory
  • Humorous but serious advice:
    1. The smaller the winning coalition, the fewer people the dicator needs to satisfy to remain in control.
    2. The larger the selectorate, the easier it is to replace dissenters.
    3. Extract as much wealth as you can without provoking rebellion or recession.
    4. Give your essential supporters just enough rewards to keep them loyal.
    5. Do not reward your supporters too well or they will become a threat.
